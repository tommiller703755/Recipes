Garlic Bread

DESCRIPTION and obligatory blog story

Ingredients:

  • Fresh garlic
  • Butter
  • French Bread
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Make and brush the garlic butter

    Preheat the over to 350Â°F. In a small bowl, combine the butter and garlic. Brush the garlic butter generously across the cut sides of the bread.
    Sprinkle with parsley. Place the bread, cut side up, on a baking sheet.

  2. Step 2: Bake the garlic bread

    Bake for eight minutes. Then turn on the broiler and set the pan 4 to 6 inches from the heat for two minutes or until the bread is golden brown. Slice and serve warm.
