Lemon Veggie Pasta

This vibrant lemon veggie pasta is a refreshing and wholesome dish that combines tender rotini pasta with a colorful medley of fresh vegetables and juicy chicken. Tossed in a zesty lemon dressing, the pasta is perfectly balanced with bright citrus flavors and a hint of garlic. The crispy chicken adds a savory, protein-packed element, while the vegetables—think bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes—bring a touch of sweetness and crunch. This dish is light, satisfying, and perfect for any season, offering a delicious way to enjoy a nutritious meal full of bright, vibrant flavors.

Ingredients:

    Roasted Vegetables

  • 12 ounces of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 small zuchhini, halved and slices
  • ½ bunch of asparagus, cut into 1" pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • ¼ teaspoon of pepper

    • Noodles

  • 1 pound of rotini
  • 1 cup of preserved pasta water
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • ¼ cup of lemon juice (or about 1-2 lemons)
  • 1½ teaspoons of fresh thyme (or ½ teaspoon dry thyme)
  • 1 tablespoon of finely chopped fresh oregano (or 1 teaspoon of dry oregano)
  • ¼ cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup of fresh basil, chopped

    • Garnish (optional)

  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Crumbled bacon

Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Roasted Vegetables: Preheat the oven to 400°F. In a medium bowl, toss vegetables and garlic with olive oil, sugar, salt and pepper until well combined (there will be a generous amount of oil - this will transfer to the pasta later). Spread vegetables on a baking sheet lined with a slip mat or parchment paper. Roast for 15 minutes or until asparagus is crisp-tender.

  2. Step 2:

    Pasta: Meanwhile, cook pasta al denta in generously salted water. Drain and reserve 1 cup of pasta water. Add the pasta to a very large mixing bowl or pot.

  3. Step 3:

    Chicken: Meanwhile, remove chicken from marinade and proceed to grill chicken or cook indoors by heating 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken and cook for 3-5 minutes or until well browned on one side. Flip the chicken over, reduce heat to medium, cover and cook an for additional 5-7 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through (time will depend on the chicken's thickness). Remove it to a cutting board to rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

  4. Step 4:

    Assemble: Add the red onion to the same skillet the chicken was cooked in (add additional olive oil if necessary). Saute onions over medium heat until soft and translucent, approximately 5 minutes. Remove the red onion to cooked pasta, along with thyme, oregano, parsley, basil, lemon juice, and roasted vegetables. Toss to combine. Add the pasta water from earlier (a little at a time) to reach the desired consistency. Taste and add additional salt and pepper, and red pepper is desired.

  5. Step 5 (optional):

    Garnish with freshly grated parmesan and bacon.
