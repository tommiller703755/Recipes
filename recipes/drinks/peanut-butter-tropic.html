Peanut Butter Tropic

This drink comes courtesy of the Schooner Bar aboard Royal Carribean Cruise ships. I've always been a fan of Skrewball, so when I found out they had a drink built around it I knew I had to give it a try, and I sure wasn't disappointed.

Ingredients:

  • 1Â½ ounces of Skrewball Peanut Butter Whiskey
  • 2 ounces of Pineapple Juice
  • 1 ounce of Lemon Juice
  • 2 dashes of Angostura Bitters

Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Combine all the ingredients in a shaker, along with ice. Shake vigurously until thoroughly mixed.

  2. Step 2:

    Add fresh ice to a glass and strain the cocktail from the shaker into the glass.

  3. Step 3:

    Garnish with a lemon wedge and enjoy.
