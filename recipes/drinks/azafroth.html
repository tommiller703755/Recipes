Azafroth

Here we have a drink themed around Azathoth, one of the deities from H.P. Lovecraft's Cthulhu Mythos. I've been a fan of the Cthulhu Mythos and the themes of cosmic horror it pioneered for a very long time, so when I saw this drink I knew I had to include it

Funnily enough despite being named after a deity best known for sleeping, this drink is certain to keep you up, with two full tablespoons of espresso, and three ounces of coffee liqueur.

    Ingredients:

  • 1½ tablespoons boiling hot water
  • 2 tablespoons instant espresso
  • ½ tablespoon black food coloring
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 ounces whole milk
  • ¼ teaspoon neon green food coloring
  • 3 ounces Kahlua coffee liqueur
  • Ice

    Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Add the black food coloring, and brown sugar into the boiling water. Mix until you see stiff peaks forming.

  2. Step 2:

    Transfer this into a shaker with the milk, the neon green food coloring, and the Kahlua. Shake until thoroughly mixed.

  3. Step 3:

    Pour from the shaker into a glass and top with the instant espresso.
