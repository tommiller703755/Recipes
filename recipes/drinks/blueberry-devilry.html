Blueberry Devilry

Here we have a wickedly refreshing cocktail that's sure to tempt your taste buds with its bold and fruity allure. This tantalizing drink combines the sweet and tangy flavors of fresh blueberries with the smooth warmth of rum and brandy, while coconut rum adds a tropical twist. Agave syrup balances the drink's natural sweetness, while a splash of sweet and sour mix and zesty lime juice bring the perfect tang to the mix.

Served over ice, this devilishly delicious concoction is crowned with a striking garnish: a pair of watermelon "horns" that add a playful, eye-catching touch. Whether you're entertaining guests or treating yourself to a devilishly good time, Blueberry Devilry is the ultimate indulgence for those who crave both complexity and refreshment in their drinks

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of blueberries
  • 1 cup of crushed ice
  • 3 ounces of rum
  • 2 ounces of brandy
  • 1 ounce of coconut rum
  • ¼ ounce of agave syrup
  • ½ ounce of sweet and sour mix
  • ½ ounce of lime juice
  • Watermelon "horns" to garnish

    Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Mix all the ingredients together in a blender, blending until smooth.

  2. Step 2:

    Add in two watermelon "horns" as a garnish
