Garlic Bread

Garlic bread is the ultimate comfort food, perfectly combining crispy, buttery toast with the rich, aromatic flavors of roasted garlic and fresh herbs. A golden-brown crust gives way to a soft, pillowy interior, while a generous spread of garlic butter — often accented with parsley, oregano, and a hint of parmesan — elevates every bite. Whether served as a side dish, appetizer, or snack, garlic bread’s irresistible aroma and satisfying crunch make it a crowd-pleaser at any meal. Best enjoyed fresh from the oven, it's the perfect pairing with pasta, salads, or a hearty soup, transforming any meal into a cozy, flavor-packed experience.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh garlic
  • Butter
  • French Bread
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Make and brush the garlic butter

    Preheat the over to 350°F. In a small bowl, combine the butter and garlic. Brush the garlic butter generously across the cut sides of the bread.
    Sprinkle with parsley. Place the bread, cut side up, on a baking sheet.

  2. Step 2: Bake the garlic bread

    Bake for eight minutes. Then turn on the broiler and set the pan 4 to 6 inches from the heat for two minutes or until the bread is golden brown. Lay some parmesan cheese on the bread. Slice and serve warm.
