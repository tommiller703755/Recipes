Italian Mushrooms

Italian Mushrooms are a savory, easy-to-make appetizer featuring tender mushrooms slow-cooked with a rich blend of onions, butter, and Italian seasoning. Simply combine the ingredients in a slow cooker, and let the flavors meld together for a warm, aromatic dish that’s perfect for any gathering. With minimal prep and maximum flavor, it’s the ultimate crowd-pleaser!

    Ingredients:

  • 1 Pound medium fresh mushrooms
  • 1 Large onion, sliced
  • ½ Cup butter, melted
  • 1 Envelope italian salad dressing mix

    Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    In a 3-qt. slow cooker, layer mushrooms and onion. Combine butter and salad dressing mix; pour over vegetables. Cover and cook on low until the vegetables are tender, which should take about 4-5 hours.
