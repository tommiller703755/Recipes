Green Beans

Green Beans are a quick and delicious side dish, where fresh green beans are saut√©ed in buttery goodness with a hint of garlic for added flavor. This easy-to-make recipe enhances the beans' natural sweetness, offering a perfect balance of savory richness and aromatic depth. Ideal for any meal, these green beans are a delightful, fuss-free addition to your table.

    Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of green beans
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tbsp of garlic
  • salt

    Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Wash the green beans and chop off their ends.

  2. Step 2:

    Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.

  3. Step 3:

    Add the washed green beans to the butter and saute them.

  4. Step 4:

    Add the garlic and continue sauteing the beans.

  5. Step 5:

    Reduce the heat, cover the beans and allow them to steam for about ten minutes.

  6. Step 6:

    Sprinkle some salt on the beans and right away, they're best hot!
